Research

Understanding

We all have mental health and we all go through periods in our life when our mental health is affected. It's a global public health problem. Symptoms such as stress, anxiety, burnout, loneliness or depression have been growing among the population.

The Challenge

Spotify is a well stablished streaming platform with a great social influence and privilege position. With the globally increase of problems associated with mental health and well-being, music gained an important role as a tool for overcoming.

The Segment

For young adults (18-30) knowing what to do to positively relieve emotional tension, this can be a complex task and often without an obvious solution.
